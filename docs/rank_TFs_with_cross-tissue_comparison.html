Rank TFs with cross-tissue comparison

Motivation

We expected to see TFs with high regulatory activity have higher expression changes when comparing across tissue. From the scatter plot, we observed many TFs do not follow this trend. One problem is the y-axis represents the difference in target counts, not informative to show how the TF target genes differ.
